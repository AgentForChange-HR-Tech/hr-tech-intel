## Key Frameworks for 2026

**Systemic HR** — HR as an integrated operation, not siloed centers of excellence. Bersin's foundational model for the AI era.

**Superagents** — AI systems that stitch together functional agents to manage entire HR processes end-to-end, from recruiting to offboarding.

**Superworker** — The human + AI collaboration model where individuals are augmented to perform at dramatically higher levels.

**5-Layer Agent Architecture** — Systems of record → workflow/experience → agents → superagents → platforms (Galileo, Copilot Studio, Joule Studio).

**HR 2030 Vision** — Bersin's forward model: agents with full employee data context, connected to all business systems, replacing multi-level review cycles.
