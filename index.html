Key frameworks to know
Systemic HR
HR as integrated operation, not siloed COEs. Bersin's foundational model for 2026.
Superagents
AI systems that stitch together functional agents to manage entire HR processes end-to-end.
Superworker
Human + AI collaboration model where individuals are augmented to perform at dramatically higher levels.
5-Layer Agent Architecture
Systems of record → workflow → agents → superagents → platforms (Galileo, Copilot Studio, Joule).
HR 2030 Vision
Bersin's forward model: agents with full employee data context, connected to all business systems.
